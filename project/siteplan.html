Site Plan

Bordados Sayde Store

The name “Bordados Sayde” reflects the store’s focus on indigenous cultural clothing, highlighting the traditional embroidery that represents culture, heritage, and elegance in its garments.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase and sell indigenous clothing, allowing customers to discover our authentic products and easily place orders from the comfort of their homes.
